Discover Sri Lanka with Safari Tours

"With over 18 years of unforgettable safari experiences, we are Sri Lanka’s oldest and most trusted adventure guide—where every journey becomes a memory."

At Safari Tours Sri Lanka, we specialize in creating thrilling, authentic, and safe safari adventures. For over 18 years, our expert guides have led thousands of travelers through Sri Lanka's most breathtaking national parks, cycling trails, and coastal skies. Whether it's a rugged jeep safari, a peaceful ride through the wilderness, or soaring in a seaplane, we turn each trip into a memorable story. Join us for a journey into the wild, where nature meets experience and every moment is unforgettable.
